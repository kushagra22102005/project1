The human body has 650+ distinct muscles, but you don’t need to memorize them all to master weight training.


Sometimes less is more. That’s why we’re simplifying things to just 11 easy-to-remember muscle groups.


You’ll also find the best tips for each muscle group–derived from cutting-edge science, world-class coaches, and practical real-world experience.


Follow the advice laid out in this article to achieve a fit, balanced physique while reducing the risk of injury.


Chest

The muscles of your chest play a key role in horizontal pushing movements as well as controlling the humerus (upper arm bone).


Best Exercise of Chest which focuses on deep stretch and over all chest development.

  1. Inclined Bench Press (Alternative:-Incline Dumbell Press, Smith Machine Incline Press)
  2. Flat Bench Press(Alternative:-Flat Dumbell Press, Smith Machine Flat Bench Press)
  3. Seated Cable Fly(Alternative:-Pec Dec Machine Fly)

Back

Back are star players for performance as well as posture.When we say back, we’re actually talking about everything between your neck and butt on the rear of your body. And as you probably realize, there are several muscle groups in that region.


  1. Lat-Pull Down (Close Grip focuses more on Lower Lats, Wide Grip focus more on Upper Lats)
  2. Bent-Over Rowig(Alternative:-Chest supported rowing, T-bar Row, Seated Cable-Rowing)
  3. Pull over
  4. Back Extension(Strenthen Lower Back)

Biceps

Your biceps brachii have two heads, short and long. They’re responsible for elbow flexion, and they act in opposition to the triceps.


Best Exercise of Biceps which focuses on deep stretch and over all Biceps development.

  1. Preacher Curls
  2. Barbell Curls(Alternative:-Dumbell or Cable Curls)
  3. Face Away Cable Curls(Best for Stretch)

Triceps

Want muscular arms? Your triceps make up two-thirds of your upper arm musculature, so start there. This three-headed muscle is also incredibly explosive and powerful, making it a top priority for athletes and strength enthusiasts.


Best Exercise of Chest which focuses on deep stretch and over all chest development.

  1. Overhead Tricep Extension
  2. Rope Push Down
  3. Close grin Bench Press

Shoulders

The glenohumeral (shoulder) joint is one of the most fragile joints in your entire body, and it’s involved in every single upper body movement. Your deltoids, the meaty round muscles on either side of your collarbone, technically have seven heads (separate bundles of muscle fibers that give the muscle its appearance). But for the purpose of selecting single-joint movements to isolate your delts, it’s easiest to think of them as having anterior (front), lateral (side), and posterior (rear) heads.


Best Exercise of Shoulders which focuses on deep stretch and over all shoulder development.

  1. Overhead Press
  2. Cable Side Raises
  3. Rear Delt Fly

Fore-Arms

Think about it: you might be able to push or pull hundreds of pounds on an evenly-balanced, 1.1-inch diameter barbell, but what about objects that aren’t designed for easy lifting?
There are three main types of grip you can train:
1.Crush grip, where your fingers flex towards your palms
2.Pinch grip, where your thumb and fingers flex towards one another
3.Support grip, where you hold a heavy object in your hands statically (think deadlifts or farmer's walks, or lifting irregular objects like boulders)


Best Exercise of Fore-Arms which focuses on deep stretch and over all Fore-Arms development.

  1. Wrist curls and reverse wrist curls
  2. Heavy partial rack deadlifts with overhand grip and no straps (bonus: use a fat bar)
  3. Pinch grip static holds using one or two weight plates

ABS

Your abs consist of the rectus abdominis, inner and outer obliques, and the hidden transversus abdominis. Collectively they flex, rotate, and stabilize your lumbar spine.


Best Exercise of Abs which focuses on deep stretch and over all Abs development.

  1. Cable Crunches
  2. Planks
  3. Leg Raises

Quadriceps

Your quadriceps or “quads,” located on the front of your thighs, get their name because they consist of four muscles that work together to extend your knees.


Best Exercise of Quads which focuses on deep stretch and over all Quads development.

  1. Barbell Squats
  2. Machine hack squat
  3. Leg Extensions

hamstrings

Your hamstrings are located on the back of your legs, where they work to extend your hips and flex your knees.


Best Exercise of which focuses on deep stretch and over all development.

  1. Seated Hamstring Curls
  2. Lying Hamstring Curls
  3. RDL's

Glutes

Your gluteus maximus is the single largest muscle in your body, as well as one of the most important for athletic performance and looking good.


Best Exercise of Glutes which focuses on deep stretch and over all Glutes development.

  1. Hip Thrust
  2. Romanian Deadlifts
  3. Hip Extensions

Calves

Your calves originate at your femur and insert into your Achilles tendon. They consist of the gastrocnemius and soleus muscles.


Best Exercise of Calves which focuses on deep stretch and over all Calves development.

  1. Standin or Sitting Calve Raises
  2. Leg press machine Calve Raises
